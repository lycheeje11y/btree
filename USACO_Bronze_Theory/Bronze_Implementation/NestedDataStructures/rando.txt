
613. Most Followers
187
Social beedia is starting to pick up in the hive. 
They are using a new platform called MyHive where bees can follow each other and leave likes and comments on their posts.
The developers of MyHive are developing a way to track which bees are following other bees 
and need your help!
Help them determine which bee has the most followers!
Input Format
The first line contains N, denoting the number of bees.
For each bee:
The first line contains their username and the number of bees they follow.
The second line contains the usernames of the bees they follow.
Output Format
Print the username of the bee with the most followers with a list of their followers in alphabetical order.
Sample Input

4
busybee 2
NoneYoBeesWax xDaRkBeEx
VIBEe 1
xDaRkBeEx
NoneYoBeesWax 2
busybee VIBEe
beeauty 4
busybee VIBEe NoneYoBeesWax xDaRkBeEx

Sample Output

xDaRkBeEx - VIBEe beeauty busybee
